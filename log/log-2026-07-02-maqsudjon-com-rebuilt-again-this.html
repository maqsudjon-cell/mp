maqsudjon.com — rebuilt again, this time as a full General Intuition homage

Tore the site down and rebuilt it in the image of generalintuition.com — the frontier lab whose whole visual language is ASCII. Everything is here: the white 1px blueprint grid that frames every section, the giant video-to-ASCII header (my F1 lap footage sampled live into coloured glyphs and tiles), the ASCII Settings panel with working Cell size / Text opacity / Tile opacity sliders, quotes that light up as you scroll, headings that de-scramble from glyph noise, invert-on-hover project tiles, a story modal (Why pangea8), and a white footer highlight card. My own touches: the wordmark is a two-line figlet MAQSUDJON rendered in block glyphs, my profile photo is re-rendered as a live ASCII portrait in the contact section, and the Music slider in the settings panel doesn't play a hidden track — it plays a tiny ambient synth generated in the browser with the Web Audio API, a nod to Matsumi. Still one self-contained HTML file, vanilla JS, no build step.
