pierics.com — polishing the Wordle clone

Spent a session refining pierics.com, my Wordle clone. Rebranded it from its working name to Pierics, wired up the pierics.com custom domain, and made the keyboard keys much bigger and more tap-friendly on phones. The word list got real attention too: I swapped out every word that wasn't exactly five letters, added around 80 common five-letter words — plurals and verbs — to the list of allowed guesses, and ended up with a clean set of 128 answer words. Small game, but the details are what make it feel right.
